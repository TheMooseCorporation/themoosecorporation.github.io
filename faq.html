FAQ

Where do I start?
Find a town or make one! This is a multiplayer pack at heart, that's been balanced around having friends. If you don't, just say that you're looking for a town and you're sure to be recruited in a flash!
Where are ores?
Ore generation has been altered with the "Geolosys" mod. You'll find ores in much larger veins, however they will be much sparser. Find a vein and fight to keep it!
How can I teleport to spawn?
There will be no /spawn or /home commands available, you'll need to find other means of transportation. Immersive railroading and vehicles helps with this.
